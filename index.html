About

Brandon is a graphic designer working across print, digital, and physical spaces, based in the Sacramento area and educated at California State University, Sacramento. For over two years he's led design for Temple Coffee Roasters, where the work spans packaging, merchandise, campaigns, and the site that sells all of it, building brand systems that hold up across every touchpoint a customer actually encounters.
