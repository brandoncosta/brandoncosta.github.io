The Designer

BC

I Make Temple
Look as Good
As It Tastes.

I'm Brandon Costa, a designer at Temple Coffee Roasters — Sacramento's specialty coffee roaster with 9 cafés spread across the region, from Sacramento to Davis and Folsom.

I work across the full brand: the packaging that earns shelf presence, the apparel and merch people actually want to wear, the signage systems that hold up across every location, the drinkware built to last, and the web presence that reflects the care we put into every roast.

Working in-house at a growing brand means covering a lot of ground — and I've embraced every part of it.
